About Kasuya

Welcome to our cozy vegetable shop nestled in the heart of Kyoto, Japan. We take pride in offering a wide variety of fresh, locally-sourced vegetables at affordable prices. Our produce is carefully selected from nearby farms, ensuring the highest quality and supporting local farmers. From crisp, green lettuce to juicy, ripe tomatoes, every item in our store reflects the rich agricultural heritage of the Kyoto region. Come visit us and enjoy the freshest flavors of Kyoto's fields, all while supporting your community and saving money.
